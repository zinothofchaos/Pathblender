Mother Vulture

The Flesheater

Source Concordance of Rivals pg. 12
Pathfinder Wiki Mother Vulture

Details

Alignment N
Pantheon Psychopomp Usher
Areas of Concern Consumption, renewal, and transformation
Domains Animal, Destruction, Healing, Repose
Subdomains N/A
* Requires the Acolyte of Apocrypha trait.
Favored Weapon Sickle
Symbol Two crossed diagonal lines with an empty circle above and a filled circle below

Obedience

Consume the flesh or blood of your own people and reflect on the pain and death required to grant strength and life; if this is your own flesh or blood, the process deals 1d6 points of damage. Gain a +4 insight bonus against effects that would cause you to become sickened or nauseated. You can survive by eating rotting and diseased food with no ill effect.

Boons - Monitor Obedience

None

Source Concordance of Rivals pg. 12
1: Carrion Eater (Sp) decompose corpse 3/day, animal aspect 2/day, or cure serious wounds 1/day
2: Devourer (Ex) You gain a bite attack that deals 1d4 points of damage (1d3 for small creatures) plus your Strength modifier. If you confirm a critical hit with this attack, it also deals 1 point of bleed damage. If you already have a bite attack, your bite deals 2 points of bleed damage on a critical hit. You are considered proficient with this attack.
3: New from Old (Sp) You can cast extended polymorph any object once per day.

Falayna

Warrior's Ring

Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 322
Pathfinder Wiki Falayna

Details

Alignment LG
Pantheon Empyreal Lords
Areas of Concern Femininity, martial training, rings
Domains Good, Law, Liberation, Strength
Subdomains Archon (Good), Archon (Law), Freedom, Resolve, Revolution, Self-Realization (Liberation)*, Self-Realization (Strength)*
* Requires the Acolyte of Apocrypha trait.
Favored Weapon Longsword
Symbol Sword held aloft
Sacred Animal(s) Cat
Sacred Color(s) Silver, white

Obedience

Don a jeweled ring on each finger and strike a practice dummy barehanded until the impression of the rings is left in your skin. Gain a +4 sacred bonus on grapple checks and to CMD.

Boons - Celestial Obedience

Mystery Cultist

Source Chronicle of the Righteous pg. 12
1: Martial Excellence (Sp) divine favor 3/day, spiritual weapon 2/day, or greater magic weapon 1/day
2: Strong Arm (Su) As a swift action, you can increase the damage die of your weapon by one step for a number of rounds per day equal to your HD. These rounds need not be consecutive.
3: Falayna's Rings (Su) Once per day, you can summon a floating array of giant iron rings that provide cover or attack foes, as if you had cast crushing hand.

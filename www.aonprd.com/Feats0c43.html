Armor Style Feats

Description Source Armor Master's Handbook pg. 10
Although most inexperienced warriors think of armor as passive protection rather than the cornerstone of a fighting style, some martial traditions make heavy use of armor. These armor fighting styles are embodied by style feats, following the usual rules for such feats. Instead of requiring Improved Unarmed Strike as a prerequisite, however, all armor fighting styles require proficiency in light, medium, or heavy armor as a prerequisite. Armor style feats can be used only while wearing armor whose type matches the feat’s armor proficiency prerequisite, disregarding any changes resulting from the armor’s material. These feats count as style feats for all other purposes.
